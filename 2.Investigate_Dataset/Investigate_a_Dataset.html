Introduction¶

I have chosen TMDB movie data set for this project.
This data set contains information about 10,000 movies collected from The Movie Database (TMDb).

Independent variables thet will be explored:

  • Budget (How the budget changes over years)
  • Revenue (How the revenue changes over years)
  • Runtime (What is the average runtime of movies)

Dependent variables thet will be explored:

  • Budget and popularity, revenue and popularity, runtime and popularity

Conclusions¶

  • The budget and revenue of movies are increasing over years
  • The analysis of relation between (budget and popularity),(revenue and popularity) were made by grouping the movies under production companies
  • Both of budget and revenue have a relation with popularity (but not very significant)
  • Most of the movies are timed between 80 minutes to 120 minutes.

Limitations¶

  • We analyze budget, revenue and revenue columns were rows not zeros and this might have affected our analysis.
  • Applying descriptive statistics to draw a conclusion isn't enough, we need to apply inferential statistics in order to have a more meaningful insights
